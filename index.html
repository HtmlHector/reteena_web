AI for Alzheimer's Diagnosis & Therapy

We are building Low-Field MRI Resolution Image Enhancement Framework for Better Alzheimer's Diagnosis and the LLM-based Memory Repository for Reminiscence Therapy , submission at MIT SOLVE , sponsorship from IEEE BigData 2024 , and accomplishments behind our innovative work.

Reteena shares the same pronunciation as retina, a vital part of the eye used for vision. Like the retina, Reteena aims to provide a new perspective on Alzheimer's, illuminating paths toward effective treatment and memory management.

Research

Our research focuses on two key areas:

Low-Field MRI Resolution Enhancement

We are developing advanced deep learning models to enhance the resolution and quality of low-field MRI scans. This technology makes early Alzheimer's diagnosis more accessible by improving the diagnostic value of more affordable MRI equipment.

  • Super-resolution techniques for medical imaging
  • Artifact reduction in low-field MRI
  • CNN and transformer-based image enhancement
  • Quantitative validation with clinical data

LLM-Based Memory Repository

Our memory repository platform uses large language models to organize, enhance, and recall personal memories for Alzheimer's patients. This innovative approach supports reminiscence therapy by providing structured access to important life events and memories.

  • Personalized memory structuring
  • Multi-modal data integration (text, images, audio)
  • Contextual memory retrieval
  • Compassionate AI-guided reminiscence
